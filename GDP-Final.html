Data Visualization - Project 1

In this project, I compared education, health, military expenditures of G20 countries with their GDP.
The datasets are obtained from World Bank website but due to missing data in education dataset for a few countries in some years,
I used www.indexmundi.com to get the missing data. I visualized the data of top 10 countries in health and education expenditure from 2006 to 2014. The data of Education and Health expenditure of 2015 and 2016 were not provided in both datasets.

Conclusion:


1. USA, UK and Canada have higher GDP, education and health expenditure per capita.
2. The health and education expenditure of India, China and Russia are almost equal to their military expenditure.
3. USA and Canada have highest GDP, health expenditure and education expenditure per capita in comparison with other countries.
4. India, China and Russia have higher GDP but they spend less than other countries such as Canada on health and education.
5. Average growth percentage shows that India, China, Russia and Brazil had faster growth in both health and education expenditure.
